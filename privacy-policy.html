Privacy Policy

Learn With Fun

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

Overview

Learn With Fun ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children who use our educational app. This privacy policy explains our practices regarding data collection and use.

Information Collection

We DO NOT collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

Learn With Fun is designed as a completely offline educational app that:

  • Does not require user registration or login
  • Does not collect names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • Does not track user location
  • Does not access contacts, photos, or other personal data
  • Does not use analytics or tracking tools
  • Does not contain advertisements
  • Does not have social media integration

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on the user's device, including:

  • Game progress and scores
  • App settings and preferences

This data never leaves the device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

COPPA Compliance

This app is designed for children and fully complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not:

  • Collect personal information from children under 13
  • Share children's information with third parties
  • Allow children to make their information publicly available

Permissions

The app requests only the following permission:

  • VIBRATE: Used to provide haptic feedback during gameplay for an enhanced learning experience

No other permissions are requested or used by this app.

Third-Party Services

Learn With Fun does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app functions completely offline without any external connections.

Data Security

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no user data at risk. All gameplay data remains stored locally on the user's device using the device's built-in security features.

Children's Privacy

For Parents: We encourage parents to monitor their children's app usage and discuss online privacy and safety with them. This app is designed to be a safe, offline learning environment with no external communications or data collection.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

International Users

This app can be used globally. Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no cross-border data transfer concerns. All data remains on the user's device regardless of location.
