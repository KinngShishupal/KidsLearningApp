## Gameplay Flow

### 1. Word Appears
- See the emoji image
- See word length
- See difficulty level
- Timer starts counting

### 2. Think & Plan
- Look at scrambled letters
- Count how many letters needed
- Use hint if needed
- Plan your spelling

### 3. Build the Word
- Tap letters in order
- Letters fill boxes left to right
- Watch your progress
- Use undo for mistakes

### 4. Complete or Correct
- **Correct spelling**: Celebration! Auto-advance to next word
- **Wrong spelling**: Game ends when all boxes filled
- **Timeout**: Game ends if timer reaches 0

### 5. Results
- See final score
- View accuracy stats
- Star rating
- Options: Restart, Home, Share, Next Challenge

## Tips for Success

1. **Use the Hint**: Don't be afraid to check the hint!
2. **Count First**: See how many letters you need
3. **Look for Vowels**: A, E, I, O, U are important
4. **Use Undo**: Made a mistake? Just undo instead of clearing all
5. **Take Your Time**: Easy words have 20 seconds - plenty of time!
